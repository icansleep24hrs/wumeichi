Profile
吳美琪擅長以徒手架設不可能的鮮豔光影場景，被幻視的空間凌駕於常理之上，在切割空間維度的過程當中， 俗常的物件蛻變成為擁有強勢姿態感的靜物，創作者的姿態也如同擁有孩童心胸的造物主，玩心一開就不願放手。
Wu MeiChi specialises in handcrafting extraordinarily vivid settings of light and shadow that foreground illusionary spaces. In these spatial dimensions, existing familiar objects are transformed into peculiar still lifes. With a whimsical attitude, she possesses a childlike, innocent stubbornness: one that is reluctant to let go of play.
